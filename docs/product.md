# StockPilot

## Problem

Small businesses often struggle to manage inventory efficiently due to manual tracking, spreadsheets, and lack of real-time visibility. This leads to stock shortages, overstocking, expired products, and inefficient warehouse management.

## Solution

StockPilot is a modern inventory and warehouse management platform that helps businesses manage products, categories, suppliers, warehouses, and stock movements through a clean and intuitive dashboard. It also provides low-stock notifications and expiry alerts for products where expiration tracking is applicable, helping businesses reduce waste and prevent losses.

## Target Users

- Pharmacy Owners
- Retail Stores
- Grocery Stores
- Electronics Shops
- Clothing Stores
- Cosmetic Stores
- Small Warehouses

## Goals

- Simplify inventory management
- Track stock across warehouses
- Manage suppliers and categories
- Monitor low stock levels
- Track product expiry where applicable
- Reduce inventory loss
- Provide actionable business insights through dashboards
